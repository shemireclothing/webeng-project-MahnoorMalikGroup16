How It Works

Our streamlined process makes it easy to report, track, and resolve utility complaints. Follow these simple steps to get your issue resolved quickly.

1

Register Your Complaint

Fill out a simple online form with details about your utility issue. Include your location, complaint type, and description.

  • Choose the utility service type
  • Provide your contact information
  • Describe the issue in detail
  • Upload photos if needed
  • Submit your complaint
📄
2

Complaint Verification

Our system automatically verifies your complaint and assigns it to the relevant department.

  • Automatic location verification
  • Department assignment
  • Priority level assessment
  • Acknowledgment receipt sent
  • Unique tracking ID generated
✅
3

Issue Resolution

The assigned department works on resolving your complaint. You receive real-time updates throughout the process.

  • Field team dispatched
  • Regular status updates sent
  • Progress tracked in dashboard
  • Estimated resolution time shared
  • Work order managed
🔧
4

Feedback & Closure

Once resolved, you receive a notification and can provide feedback on the resolution quality.

  • Resolution notification sent
  • Satisfaction survey
  • Rate the service
  • Complaint archived
  • Follow-up support available
⭐
